Who We Are

Spanda by Puja Unni is an artistic space that has nurtured some well known performers in and around Dubai.

We continue to work on enhancing the practice, understanding and appreciation for Bharatanatyam. Following the Kalakshetra based syllabus, our aim is to promote the Indian Culture and help students abroad stay well-connected to their roots.

Improves Concentration

At Spanda, we focus on a holistic learning experience which not only helps the body but also helps the mind in staying agile. Studies have proven practicing Bharatanatyam hones mental skills and builds mental alertness.

Healthy and Glowing Skin

How to get a healthy, glowing skin? Look no further. Join us for our Bharatanatyam Sessions and welcome to the world of better skin. Regular Bharatanatyam performers tend to express various emotions using their eyes, eyebrows, cheeks etc. which makes the facial muscles stronger thus improving skin texture and complexion.

Helps in Weightloss

After a certain age, the body tends to lose its metabolism and people generally begin to put on weight. Whether its your lifestyle or post-pregnancy weight or even build on your self esteem, you can shed all that weight with us and also learn a new beautiful art form.

Discipline and better social skills

Learning Bharatanatyam involves a lot of discipline, devotion and dedication. The patient practice of adavus or basic steps brings in a lot of discipline. It also involves meeting new children, and thereby improves social skills.
